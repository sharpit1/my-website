About Me

Hello! I am Dongsu Song. I received my M.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from Korea Aerospace University in 2025. My research interests lie in Trustworthy AI, Adversarial Robustness, and Reinforcement Learning.

Currently, I am focusing on enhancing the robustness of vision models against sparse adversarial attacks and exploring safety in Multi-Modal Language Models.
